## How to Use

### Step 1: First Person (Interviewer)

1. **Open** `client-standalone.html` in your browser (just double-click it)
2. **Fill in:**
   - Your Name: "John" (or whatever)
   - Role: "Interviewer"
   - Room Name: **Leave blank**
3. **Click** "Join Room"
4. **Allow** camera and microphone when asked
5. **Copy** the room name that appears (like "interview-2024-11-15-143000")

### Step 2: Second Person (Candidate)

1. **Open** `client-standalone.html` in another browser window
   - Or send the file to a friend and they open it
   - Or open in incognito/private window
2. **Fill in:**
   - Your Name: "Jane" (or whatever)
   - Role: "Candidate"
   - Room Name: **Paste the room name from Step 1**
3. **Click** "Join Room"
4. **Allow** camera and microphone when asked

## Notes

- The credentials are **already pre-filled** in the HTML file

- This connects directly to LiveKit Cloud
- Works completely offline (except for LiveKit connection)

## Controls

Once connected:
- **Toggle Microphone** - Mute/unmute your mic
- **Toggle Camera** - Turn camera on/off
- **Leave Room** - Disconnect

## Sharing with Team

Just send them the `client-standalone.html` file. They can:
1. Open it in their browser
2. Enter the same room name
3. Join the call!

## Troubleshooting

**Camera/Mic not working?**
- Make sure you clicked "Allow" in the browser
- Try Chrome or Firefox (best compatibility)
- Check your camera/mic aren't being used by another app

**Can't see other person?**
- Make sure you both entered the **exact same room name**
- Check both people clicked "Join Room"
- Look at the status message for errors

**Video is laggy?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try closing other applications
- Reduce number of participants
